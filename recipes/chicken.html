Garlic Fried Chicken

Description

The following is a recipe taken from allrecipes.com to make garlic fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 4 skinless, bonless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs and flour. In a seperate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.
  2. Heat the oil in an electric skillet set to 350 degrees F. Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.
  3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a slotted spatula, and serve.
